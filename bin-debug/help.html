Ramune was designed, engineered, and constructed by Hector G. Parra, Project Software Engineer at the University of California, Irvine division of the California Institute for Telecommunications & Information Technology (Calit2).

Ramune is a soda pop from Japan. It's easily recognized by it's codd neck bottle, which contains a marble to prevent gas from escaping. They come in various tasty flavors. The most popular brands are Shirakiku and Sangaria. In Orange County, CA it can be purchased from 99 Ranch Market, H-Mart, and Mitsuwa.
